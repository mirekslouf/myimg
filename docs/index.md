### Selected examples for quick start

* [01_images.nb.pdf](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1abxWcKD9GOGtMYASjO67RaUsUQfJN8DO/view?usp=sharing)
  = Jupyter notebook converted to PDF; <br>
  the processing of single image(s) in MyImg
  (auto-enhance contrast, insert label, scalebar ...).
* [02_reports.nb.pdf](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1PvjTpaPVBNYZf-5DiYkJ2um07XuO5esY/view?usp=sharing) 
  = Jupyter notebook converted to PDF; <br>
  the preparation of high-quality tiled images in MyImg,
  perfectly aligned and ready for a presentation or publication.
* [03_fft.nb.pdf](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1owEgegyk7OgwbLo2RFF7Lem_y9wE90GE/view?usp=sharing)
  = Jupyter notebook converted to PDF; <br>
  a demo showing one of the additional MyImg applications - FFT utilities.
  With FFT utilities you can calculate 2D Fast Fourier transform of an image
  and analyze it by means of radial and azimuthal profiles.
